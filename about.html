About the Founders

Saath Saath Foundation (SSF), was founded in 2021, by Mona Almeida, Usha Donolikar & Razia Pathan with a mission to align volunteers who have the willingness to offer service and the Institutions who are in need of the service. Service can be in any form and for any period - intent, compassion and willingness to serve is the only prerequisite to become a Volunteer. SSF aspires to grow into becoming a movement of volunteerism, a movement that will create a life-long impact on both the volunteers and NGO they serve.

Mona Almeida

With a post graduation in development management from the prestigious S. P. Jain Institute of Management Studies, Mona Almeida has worked and contributed actively in the development field for over 10 years. She has not only been a trustee of an NGO ( that supports trafficking survivors and women in distress) for over 8 years but also comes with corporate and entrepreneurial experience. With her leadership skills and excellent communication skills, Mona has led teams of dedicated professionals and overseen volunteer management. She herself has been a passionate volunteer for the past 20 years working in various fields from child education to women rehabilitation.

Usha Donolikar

Usha brings with her 20 years of corporate experience into the social sector. She has herself been a volunteer and contributed over the last 6 years to the community. Her compassion and dedication can be observed in her day to day working. Not only is she great at relationship management, accounting and administration, she has a close connection at the grassroot level. Persistent and hard working, Usha has supported and brought remarkable results to individual beneficiaries when the challenges were high and hope was low.

Razia Pathan

Working from the ground up, Razia Pathan is an inspiration. She has worked hard and achieved so much both professionally and personally despite her challenges. With her zeal and enthusiasm to make a difference, she has invested her life to work for the community. She has a plethora of experience (over 15 years) working at the grass-root levels with multiple not-for-profit organizations in the area of rehabilitation, children welfare and women empowerment. She envisions SSF to start a journey that brings lots of prosperity to the people working with SSF as well as the people SSF is working for.
