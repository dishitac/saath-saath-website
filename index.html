Together, we make a difference

Anyone, regardless of their background, could be a volunteer as long as they possessed genuine intention, boundless compassion, and an unwavering commitment to serve.

Saath Saath foundation is a registered non profit with all certifications like 80g and 12AA in place.

We have been working in and around Mumbai and Thane but mainly focusing in the adivasi villages of karjat, namely Chaudharwadi, Mograz, Ambivali, Pinglas and Dhamni. These are remote location, 25 kms in the interiors with barely one bus service to connect them to the station (which often gets cancelled!) and no tap connection in the houses.

Our work in these areas is three pronged.

  • Education : Here, we are a working in conjunction with Zilla parishad schools and Ashram shala school, run by the adivasi board. We have appointed a support teacher as requested by the school and also handed over computers for their use, conducted sessions on good touch, bad touch. A lot more needs to be done, nevertheless!
  • We are working in the community, for livelihood opportunities . Training young girls to stitch bags. We do need support here for paying salary to the tailoring teachers as also to help us sell the bags which the girls make.
  • Nutrition : We are also working in coordination with the local public health center and hope to make a difference as far as malnutrition is concerned, especially amongst the expecting mothers and the lactating ones too.This work is in coordination with the local Public Health Centre.

    Vision

    To create a world where every individual, regardless of their background, has equal access to opportunities, love, and a chance to thrive.

    Mission

    At (SSF), our mission is to empower marginalized communities, especially the tribal population, through education, healthcare, and sustainable development. We foster unity, celebrate diversity, and create a world where hearts connect, ensuring that no one is left behind.

    Philosophy

    At Saath Saath, we believe in harnessing the power of those who have, whether it's knowledge, skills, resources, or wisdom, to uplift those who aspire to have more. We are driven by a deep-rooted belief that altruism resides within us all, motivating us to help and share our knowledge with others.

    In a world brimming with untapped potential, we recognize the need for a conduit, and that's where Saath Saath comes in. Our mission is to facilitate a transformation, making volunteering a seamless and integral part of everyday life.

    We understand that misconceptions surround volunteering and the social sector. Some may fear that it's solely about poverty, sorrow, and disease. We're here to change that perception. For us, it's about the joy of giving and receiving. It's a dynamic exchange where everyone benefits.

    Join us in shaping a world where giving back is not an occasional act but a way of life. Discover the true essence of volunteering and the social sector with Saath Saath. Together, let's bridge the gap and create a world where knowledge, skills, and compassion flourish in harmony.

    • FAQ

    1What all do you mean by volunteering?

    Volunteering at our organization encompasses various opportunities, including:

  • Pro Bono Work: Offering your professional skills and expertise to support our mission.
  • Community Service: Participating in local initiatives, events, and outreach programs that benefit our communities.
  • Work with Expectation of Being Paid: Some roles within our organization offer paid positions for specific responsibilities.
    • 2 Can I volunteer online? Are there online opportunities available?

    Absolutely! We have online volunteering opportunities, such as helping with social media updates, document preparation, and serving as ambassadors to promote our work.

    3Will I receive a certificate for my volunteer work? Is it volunteering or an internship? How do you differentiate?

    We provide certificates for volunteer contributions. Differentiating between volunteering and internships, volunteer roles can be long-term, subject to availability. Internships are typically for students and align with their university's suggested time frame.

    4 What is the minimum donation amount you accept?

    No donation is too small; every contribution matters! We also have ongoing programs with specific requirements; feel free to contact us for details.

    5 Do you accept in-kind donations

    Yes, we do accept in-kind donations based on ongoing campaigns and program needs. Please consider the dignity of the recipient when making in-kind contributions.
