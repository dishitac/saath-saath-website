Impact stories - Volunteers

Research suggests that volunteers aren’t just helping the communities they serve. People who volunteer actually experience a boost in their mental health. This and many other benefits have been communicated by our volunteers on the effect volunteering at SSF has on them. Sharing a few testimonials for your perusal.

Testimonial 1 - Prajkta Deole

“Working with Saath Saath Foundation gives me happiness and motivation. Connecting people of different skill sets with the foundation and their efforts in each session is really appreciable. I enjoy attending the sessions with children and seeing their enthusiasm and open learning. It boosts me up to reach more volunteers.”

Testimonial 2 - Dr Savitri Gorakh

"Working with Saath Saath foundation, I have realized the world is full of wonderful people who can always put a smile on my face, be it the founders or the children I interact with. It gives me immense joy, and the kids really make my day always."

Testimonial 3 - Shekhar Shrikhande

"I have been a volunteer with SSF since last year. The service SSF does for the underprivileged and needy through NGOs is admirable. It gives me immense joy and satisfaction to contribute my mite for uplifting someone without expecting anything in return. I cannot describe the happiness I see in the eyes of the children in the classroom."
