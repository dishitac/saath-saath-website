Our Reach

Currently we work with NGO partners across Mumbai, Thane, Ulhasnagar, Kalwa, Airoli, and Pune, who work for a variety of causes ranging from child welfare, skill development, woman empowerment to rehabilitation of abuse survivors.

In a short span of a few months we have aligned 40 individual volunteers and three teams of college students all of whom come with diverse backgrounds and skill sets. They have conducted over 170 sessions in topics ranging from spoken English, yoga, dance, life skills, embroidery, to rehabilitation.

We are proud to share our journey of making a positive impact in some of the most remote and underserved areas around Mumbai and Thane. Our primary focus lies in the adivasi regions of Dhamni, Chaudharwadi, Mograz, and Pinglas in Karjat. These are truly off-the-grid locations, situated 25 kilometers deep within the interiors. To give you a glimpse of the challenges these communities face, there's barely one bus service connecting them to the station, and even that is often unreliable. Moreover, access to basic amenities like tap water is a luxury these households cannot afford.

In these far-flung regions, we have been collaborating with Zilla Parishad schools and Ashram Shala schools, which are managed by the Adivasi Board. Our aim is to bring about meaningful change and provide opportunities for growth.

One noteworthy initiative has been in the community of Ambivali, where we are creating livelihood opportunities. The community here, while slightly better off, faces a unique challenge. Young girls have received some level of education, but they are hesitant to engage in menial labor, and their parents are reluctant to send them further away from home for work. However, all these girls share a common interest in stitching, with some possessing basic skills and others eager to learn.

To address this, we have a dedicated lady ready to impart her knowledge in stitching, and we have secured a suitable space for training. What's truly groundbreaking is our partnership with a fashion house from Bangalore, which has pledged to supply us with excess cloth for training purposes. With the acquisition of four sewing machines, we can equip two of them for the benefit of this community, while the other two can be utilized for the Ashram Shala school. This will enable us to kickstart skill training programs for the young girls, a request that came directly from the school's Principal and warden.

It's important to note that we are the only nonprofit organization operating in these remote areas, making our work all the more vital and impactful. Together, we are bridging gaps and creating opportunities where they are needed most. Join us in our mission to bring hope and change to these deserving communities.

We work at 3 levels:

  • Health & Nutrition: Supporting pregnant/lactating mothers and malnourished children.
  • Education: Aiding Zilla Parishad and Ashram Shala schools with resources and support.
  • Skill Development: Training young women to craft bags for livelihood opportunities.

  • Hence, we have been actively working with the children of Chaudharwadi primary school and pre-primary children of Chaudharwadi Aanganwadi.

    Bal Snehalay (Thane)-

  • We have primarily concentrated on providing individual academic sessions for these children.
  • Our volunteers impart knowledge in various subjects for grades 6 to 12. Additionally, our volunteers also organize yoga and spoken English classes.
  • These sessions have contributed to boosting their confidence.
  • Thanks to our donor partners, we have organized Diwali and Christmas parties.
  • Our continuous efforts are put to make the important days in their lives special, and give as much exposure to various activities as possible.

    Children of God Foundation (Kalwa and Kurla)-

  • We have concentrated on providing academic sessions for the children of age 12 onwards, residing in the slum area, who have had to drop out of school for various reasons.
  • Our volunteers impart knowledge in all subjects selected according to the NIOS board of education. We prepare them for the board examination of 10th and 12th grade, so that they can complete their basic education.
  • Additionally, our volunteers also organize yoga and spoken English classes.
  • Thanks to the support of our donor partners, we are able to donate stationary items, notebooks, computers and its accessories, and everything that they might need for smoother learning experience.

    Rescue Foundation (Kandivali and Pune)-

  • SSF focuses on the rescue and rehabilitation of individuals who have been victims of trafficking (majors or minors), by conducting tapping sessions for the survivors.
  • Our volunteers have also conducted and will keep conducting sessions on academics, yoga and Bharatanatyam, to help them come out of their untold trauma.
  • To make them more self-reliant, a volunteer entrepreneur trained the young women to make products and guided them on how to market it.
  • We recently finished a project where the survivors finished stitching cloth bags, which were to be donated in communities of Mumbai, further promoting sustainable ways of living.

    Saad Foundation (Panvel)-

  • SSF concentrates on promoting the comprehensive development of children aged 6 to 14 years, thus contributing to establishing a caring and wholesome society.
  • SSF volunteers have conducted educational sessions for children on diverse topics, including personal hygiene, COVID norms and precautions, knowledge on the human body, cognitive development via puzzles and science experiments, fitness via Zumba, and much more.
  • We have partnered with professionals from various fields to talk to the children about a gamut of career opportunities, including army personnel, doctors, and offbeat professions like Zumba.
  • We also organized a picnic to Xenos Play Space at Ghatkopar for 31 children, with the assistance of donors and volunteers.
  • Additionally, with the help of donor partners, we could host several pizza parties, Christmas lunches, Diwali breakfasts, and provid essentials such as groceries, a carrom board, CPUs, etc.
